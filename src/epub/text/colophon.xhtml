In Search of the Castaways
was published between 1867 and 1868 by
Jules Verne.
It was translated from French in 1874 by
J. B. Lippincott & Co.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
Geneviève Ségol,
and is based on a transcription produced in 2014 by
Ema Majhut, Marc D’Hooghe, and Distributed Proofreaders
for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans from
Google Books.

The cover page is adapted from
Ship on the Touques,
a painting completed between 1888 and 1895 by
Eugène Boudin.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
